# ch10_fluid_crystallized_adulthood.svg

## Caption
Cognitive aging is a trade-off, not uniform decline. Fluid intelligence - novel problem solving, speed, and working memory - peaks early and declines with age. Crystallized intelligence - knowledge, vocabulary, and expertise - rises through adulthood and stays high into later life. Curves are schematic, illustrating the Horn and Cattell distinction.

## Alt text
A line chart plots ability level against age from 20 to 80. The fluid-intelligence curve peaks in early adulthood and declines, more steeply late in life. The crystallized-intelligence curve rises through adulthood, overtakes fluid intelligence, and remains high into older age.
