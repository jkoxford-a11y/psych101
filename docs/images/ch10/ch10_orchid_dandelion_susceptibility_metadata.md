# ch10_orchid_dandelion_susceptibility.svg

## Caption
Dandelion children develop reasonably well across a wide range of environments. Orchid children are more sensitive in both directions: they tend to fare worse in adverse environments but better in supportive environments. The same sensitivity is a liability in bad conditions and an advantage in good ones.

## Alt text
A line chart plots developmental outcome against environment quality from adverse to supportive. The dandelion line slopes gently and stays near the middle of the outcome range. The orchid line slopes steeply, starting below the dandelion line in adverse conditions and ending above it in supportive conditions, with a clear crossover.
