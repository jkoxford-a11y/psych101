What Does the Evidence Actually Show?

Consciousness research often begins with striking cases. The difficult step is deciding what each case actually demonstrates—and what would be an inference beyond the evidence.

    3–4. Explain the Pattern

    A Dissociation Is Not a Complete Theory

    A finding can establish a dissociation without identifying the complete mechanism. It can also fit several competing theories. Consistent with is weaker than uniquely predicted by, and neither means proved.

    Processing means a system responded to information. Attention prioritizes information or tasks. Conscious access or report means information is available for deliberate use or communication. Subjective experience is what a state feels like from the inside. They interact, but they are not synonyms.
