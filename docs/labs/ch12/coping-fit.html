Learning Lab - Chapter 12

Coping Fit: Match the First Move

Coping is not a contest between two personality types. It is a fit problem: what can change, how activated are you right now, and which move makes the next useful move possible?

Allow about 10–15 minutes. This activity practices a decision rule; it does not measure or diagnose you.

Privacy and Accessibility

This lab runs entirely in your browser. It stores only your prediction, progress, classifications, explanation, and transfer response in sessionStorage for this browser session. It stores no names, emails, IDs, health information, analytics, or tracking data, and nothing is sent to a server.

    Phase 3 of 6 - Concept Reveal

    What the Pattern Shows

    The coping-fit rule

    Problem-focused coping fits a changeable stressor when arousal permits effective action. Emotion-focused coping fits an unchangeable demand, or it can come first when high arousal blocks useful action. The categories name targets, not people. Good coping can switch targets as the situation changes.
