Correlation vs. Causation Classifier

Each headline below is fictional. First predict whether the headline's unseen evidence can support its causal claim. This initial prediction records the impression created by the headline; it is not scored. Then reveal the study design and make three separately scored judgments. Reverse causation and third variables are not competing answer choices: an observational study can leave either concern, both, or neither.

Accessibility and Privacy

This lab runs entirely in your browser. It stores predictions, judgments, and written responses in sessionStorage for this browser session so a refresh does not erase your work. It does not store names, emails, student IDs, analytics, or identifying information, and it sends no responses over a network.
