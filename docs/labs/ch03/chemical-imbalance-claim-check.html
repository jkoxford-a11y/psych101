Learning Lab - Chapter 3

Chemical Imbalance Claim-Check

Brain chemistry matters. The misconception is the simpler claim that a psychological disorder is caused by one missing chemical, and that a helpful medication proves that original cause. This lab asks you to separate careful claims from claims that overreach.

Summary and Transfer

Key Takeaways

  1. Medication effects do not prove that a disorder was caused by a simple chemical deficiency.
  2. Brain chemistry matters, but it acts through receptors, circuits, time course, body state, development, cognition, social context, and environment.
  3. Careful neuroscience claims usually avoid single-cause explanations and ask what system, context, and evidence are involved.
