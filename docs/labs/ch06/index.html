Learning Labs - Chapter 6

Sleep

These labs focus on two different reasoning problems: separating the mechanisms that determine sleepiness and using converging physiological evidence to identify a sleep state.

Mechanism and variable manipulation

When Will You Feel Sleepy?

Separate homeostatic sleep pressure from circadian timing, then test what caffeine, naps, travel, and light actually change.

Classification and misconception correction

Sleep-State Detective

Combine EEG, eye movement, muscle activity, timing, and behavior to distinguish sleep stages, parasomnias, and cataplexy.
